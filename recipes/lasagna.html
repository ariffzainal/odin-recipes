Brief of Lasagna

This lasagna recipe features layers of savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella cheese, all nestled between sheets of pasta. It's a comforting dish that's perfect for feeding a crowd and can be made ahead of time or frozen for later enjoyment.

Ingredients

  • 500grams of Minced Meat: Lean Beef with minimal fat to reduce calories
  • Onion and Garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Canned Tomatoes and Tomato Puree: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna Noodles: Use store-bought.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

Steps

  1. The meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

Remember to share with your loved ones. Do not throw excess away!
